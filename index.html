Heading Tag

Heading tags in HTML are used to define headings and subheadings on a web page. They help structure content, improve readability, and enhance search engine optimization(SEO).HTML provides 6 levels of heading tags ranging from H1(most important) to H6 (least important).
H1-Main title of the page(typically used once)
H2- Secondary headings
H3-Subheadings
H4-Sub-subheadings
H5-Minor headings
H6-Least important headings
